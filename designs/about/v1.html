Technical Overview

What is IML?
How It Works.

In-Mould Labelling (IML) is a revolutionary manufacturing process where the label becomes an integral part of the packaging. Unlike traditional methods, IML makes branding inseparable from the container, resulting in a seamless, high-durability finish that withstands extreme conditions while maintaining premium visual clarity.

The IML Transformation

A Three-Step Precision Cycle

layers

1. Pre-Printed Label

High-resolution polypropylene labels are precisely positioned inside the moulding tool prior to injection.

arrow_forward
precision_manufacturing

2. Injection Moulding

Molten plastic is injected, fusing with the label. The heat and pressure create a permanent molecular bond.

arrow_forward
check_circle

3. Finished Container

A seamless, fully recyclable unit emerges. The label is no longer a sticker; it is the container itself.
