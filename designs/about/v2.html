About Taigus – IML Container Manufacturer in Ahmedabad, Gujarat

We are a modern, technology-driven manufacturer dedicated to elevating packaging standards through precision engineering.

Located in Ahmedabad, Gujarat, Taigus combines advanced robotic manufacturing with sustainable material science to deliver world-class food-grade containers.

The Foundation

Our Story

Taigus Pvt. Ltd. was founded on the principle of bringing extreme precision to the plastic packaging industry. What started as a vision to revolutionize the Indian market has evolved into a powerhouse of manufacturing excellence.

Our state-of-the-art facility in Bavla, Ahmedabad, is designed for scale and hygiene. Every square inch of our production floor is optimized for the creation of high-impact, food-grade IML containers that meet the rigorous demands of global brands.

By focusing exclusively on In-Mold Labeling (IML) technology, we ensure that every container we produce is not just a vessel, but a high-definition marketing tool for our clients.
