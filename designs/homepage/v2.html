Who We Are

Taigus is India’s premier specialist in high-fidelity In-Mould Labelling (IML) technology. Based in Bavla, Gujarat, our state-of-the-art facility caters to the world's most demanding food and packaging brands.

We combine advanced European robotic automation with indigenous engineering precision to deliver containers that aren't just packaging—they are brand experiences.

Through decades of expertise, we have mastered the fusion of aesthetic excellence with industrial-grade durability, ensuring your products command attention on every shelf.

The IML Advantage

Why Choose IML Technology?

Unlike traditional labeling, IML fuses the label directly with the container during the injection process, creating a premium, permanent bond that is superior in every functional and aesthetic aspect.

What Is IML? How It Works

Witness the fusion of art and engineering in our 3-step production cycle.

01

Label Placement

Pre-printed polypropylene labels are positioned in the mould by robotics.

02

Injection Moulding

Molten polymer is injected, fusing permanently with the label as it cools.

03

Finished Product

A single-piece container emerges with photographic graphics and extreme durability.
