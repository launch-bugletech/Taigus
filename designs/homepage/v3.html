Our Legacy

Trusted IML Container Manufacturer for Food & Dairy Brands

Taigus is a modern packaging company based in Ahmedabad, Gujarat, specializing in In-Mould Labelling (IML) plastic containers. We combine advanced Japanese injection moulding technology with robotic automation to produce consistent, high-quality containers for ice cream, shrikhand, lassi, ghee, peanut butter, protein powder, and other food products.

Our focus is simple: Deliver food-safe, durable, and visually premium packaging that enhances brand value while ensuring product integrity and shelf life.
