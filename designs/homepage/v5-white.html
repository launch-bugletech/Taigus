FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

What is In-Mould Labelling (IML)? +
IML is a packaging technology where a pre-printed label is placed inside the mould before plastic injection. The label fuses with the container during moulding, creating a permanent, fully integrated decoration that is waterproof, durable and recyclable.
What are your minimum order quantities? +
Our standard MOQ starts at 10,000 units per SKU. For trial or sampling orders, we can accommodate smaller runs — speak to our team to discuss your requirements.
Do you manufacture containers with quality design? +
Absolutely. Our in-house design and prepress team works with your brand guidelines to prepare print-ready artwork. We also provide 3D mockups for approval before any production starts.
Do you manufacture to custom specifications? +
Yes, we offer full custom tooling and mould development. From shape to size to closure type, our engineering team will design a container that fits your exact product and filling line requirements.
Are your containers food-safe and certified? +
All our containers are manufactured using food-grade, BPA-free polypropylene and comply with FDA, EU food contact regulations, and ISO standards. Full material certification available on request.
