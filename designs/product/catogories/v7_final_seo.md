**Eyebrow:**

**H2:**
> Frequently Asked *Questions*

**Q&A Content:**

**Q1: What is the minimum order quantity (MOQ)?**
> Our standard MOQ starts at **5,000 units** for stock items. For custom IML printing with bespoke artwork, MOQ typically starts at 25,000 units depending on the container size and series.

**Q2: Are your containers microwave and freezer safe?**
> Yes. All Taigus IML containers are manufactured from high-grade virgin polypropylene (PP), which is freezer-safe to **−20°C** and microwave-safe for reheating purposes. The IML label is integrated into the container wall and remains stable across these temperature ranges.

**Q3: How long does custom IML printing take?**
> Once artwork is approved, production typically takes **15–20 business days**. We provide a digital mockup and physical press proof for sign-off before full production runs begin.

**Q4: Do you provide lid options for all container series?**
> Yes — every container series has a matching lid engineered specifically for its geometry. We offer **snap-fit**, **tamper-evident**, and **twist-lock** options depending on your product requirements and filling line specifications.

**Q5: Can you develop a custom container shape for our brand?**
> Absolutely. We offer full bespoke mould development using Japan's JSW injection moulding machinery. Our zero-human-touch process and Class 100,000 clean-room facility in Bavla, Gujarat ensures micron-level accuracy.
