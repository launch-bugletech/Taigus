Precision Engineered Packaging

The RC-500 is a flagship product in our thin-wall packaging lineup, specifically designed for the demanding requirements of the modern food industry. Crafted from high-grade, virgin Food-Grade Polypropylene (PP), this container ensures maximum safety and shelf-life for perishables.

Our In-Mold Labeling (IML) technology integrates the label directly into the wall of the container during the injection molding process. This results in a seamless, high-definition finish that is resistant to moisture, scratches, and temperature fluctuations, providing an premium brand experience that stands out on the shelf.
