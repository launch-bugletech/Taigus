Taigus offers premium IML ice cream containers meeting strict demands for safety, hygiene, and extreme freezer performance down to -18°C. Our engineering focus ensures that your brand maintains its integrity from production to the consumer's hand.

Whether you require round, square, or oval geometries, our portfolio is optimized for automated filling lines and high-impact retail display. We specialize in Thin-Wall Injection Molding (TWIM) to balance strength and weight.
