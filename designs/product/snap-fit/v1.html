What Are Snap Fit IML Containers?

Snap Fit IML (In-Mould Labelling) containers represent the pinnacle of mechanical precision in packaging. Unlike traditional friction lids, our snap-fit system features a engineered peripheral undercut that provides an audible click upon closure.

This "Mechanical Lock" ensures a tamper-evident seal that protects product integrity while the IML process fuses the label directly into the container wall during injection moulding, creating a seamless, high-definition finish that won't peel or fade.
